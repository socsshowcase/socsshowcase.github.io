It has been a busy Enhancement Week and you will have been bombarded with lots of new information and hopefully learnt lots of new things along the way! Do take this chance ot sit back, relax and join in with Chris and Friends on an informal Q&A about all things SoCS and everything in between.

  4. The link to the Twitch stream is below
  5. You can drop in and out as often as you want
  6. You don't need to register, just turn up
  7. No question is off limits or out of bounds
  8. You can ask as many questions as you want
