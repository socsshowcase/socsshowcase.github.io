Showcase Overview

The Showcase presentations and walk through's have been created by students for students. We know that many students do lots of extra-curricular projects and there are not many places to demonstrate these or show them off to peers. We wanted to change that and provide an opportunity to share, present and exchange ideas! All talks will be hosted on Teams using the SoCS Common Room. The agenda is below and we hope you enjoy each of the talks!
